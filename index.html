Research

My research interests center around, but not limited to, signal processing, machine learning, and algorithm development for physiological signals. My PhD work is in analyzing knee acoustic emissions to understand the structural integrity of the knee and to estimate joint loading which can help quantify knee joint health using wearable sensors.
