Hi. I'm Hyeon Ki Jeong.

I am a PhD candidate in school of electrical engineering at Georgia Institute of Technology. I am working with Dr. Omer Inan as a member of Inan Research Lab.

Email  /  Google Scholar  /  Linkedin

Research

My research interests center around, but not limited to, signal processing, machine learning, and algorithm development for physiological signals. My PhD work is in analyzing knee acoustic emissions to understand the structural integrity of the knee and to estimate joint loads which can help quantify knee joint health using wearable sensors.
