About Me

Hi, I am a PhD candidate in school of electrical engineering at Georgia Institute of Technology. I am working with Dr. Omer Inan as a member of Inan Research Lab on analyzing knee acoustic emissions produced from joint articulation as a wearable and quantitative biomarker to measure knee joint health. My research is focused on developing algorithms using signal processing, machine learning and/or deep learning to estimate joint contact force and quantify the direction of the asymmetry between medial and lateral compartment forces. Wearable acoustic sensing technology can potentially benefit patients by quantifying the cumulative knee usage and loading during daily activities and in real world settings.
